# Privacy Policy — SwiftPass

**Last updated:** April 2026

## Overview

SwiftPass is a Chrome extension that generates strong passwords and auto-fills them into login forms. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

SwiftPass does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All functionality runs entirely within your browser — nothing is sent to any external server.

## Permissions Used

- **activeTab** — Used only to auto-fill the generated password into the active page's password field.
- **scripting** — Used only to inject a script into the active page to locate and fill the password field.
- **clipboardWrite** — Used only to copy the generated password to your clipboard when you click Copy.
- **storage** — Used only to save your preferences (password length, character options) and recent password history locally in your browser. This data never leaves your device.
- **host_permissions** — Required to allow auto-fill to work on any website. No browsing data is accessed or recorded.

## Third Parties

SwiftPass does not use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.
